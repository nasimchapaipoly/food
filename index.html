What is Food Technology?

Food Technology is the application of food science to the selection, preservation, processing, packaging, distribution, and use of safe food.

History in Bangladesh

From traditional sun-drying and fermentation to modern high-pressure processing (HPP). The establishment of the Bangladesh Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (BCSIR) marked a turning point in local food innovation.
